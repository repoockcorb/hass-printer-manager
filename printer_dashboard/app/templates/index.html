Welcome to Printer Dashboard!

Get started by configuring your printers in Home Assistant:

  1. Go to Settings ‚Üí Add-ons
  2. Find Printer Dashboard in your installed add-ons
  3. Click on it and go to the Configuration tab
  4. Add your printers in the following format:
printers:
  - name: "Voron Klicky"
    type: "mainsail"
    url: "http://192.168.1.104"
  - name: "Prusa MK3"
    type: "octoprint"
    url: "http://192.168.1.247"

Save the configuration and restart the add-on. Your printers will appear here!
